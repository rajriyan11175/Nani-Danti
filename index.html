Nani Danti Pra. Shala was established in 1955 and it is managed by the Local body. It is located in Rural area.It is located in VALSAD block of VALSAD district of Gujrat. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. Gujarati is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by weather road.In this school academic session starts in April.The school has Government building. It has got 13 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition.The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional.The school has 2 boys toilet and it is functional. and 5 girls toilet and it is functional.The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 380 books in its library. The school has 7 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided but not Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.
